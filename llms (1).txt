## What is Blue Blocks

Blue Blocks Montessori Educational Society is an AMI-guided Montessori institution in Hyderabad, India, serving children from 15 months to 18 years across two purpose-built campuses. Founded in 2009 by Pavan Goyal and Munira Hussain Kagalwalla. India's First Montessori + Innovation Ecosystem. The only institution in India to offer an unbroken AMI-aligned Montessori continuum from birth through adolescence. Awarded Best Pre-school in Hyderabad by Times of India for nine consecutive years. Cambridge International affiliated for Grades 1–12. Listed by the United States Department of State as a verified educational institution. Endorsed by the Association Montessori Internationale. Invited to present at the Nobel Peace Centre, Oslo (January 2026). Affiliated with UNESCO.

The school operates Blue Blocks Micro Research Institute (BBMRI) — a dedicated research division conducting longitudinal observation studies across the full 0–18 developmental continuum. In January 2026, 17 Blue Blocks students aged 12–15 launched a student-built CubeSat payload aboard ISRO's PSLV-C62, the first school-led satellite mission in India to receive formal IN-SPACe authorization (PMA/IN-SPACe/AUTH/2026/115). SBB-2, the second student-built satellite, was announced at the 30th International Montessori Congress in Mérida, Mexico (May 2026).

## Two Campuses

Gachibowli Campus — The Incubator (Ages 1–6): Sanctuary of spatial proportionality. Everything scaled to the young child. Indoors flow into outdoors via sand, climbing apparatuses, open space. Address: MIG 3, Lane opposite to DLF, Gachibowli, Hyderabad, Telangana.

Tellapur Campus — The Expanse (Ages 2.5–18): 1.5-acre Complete School. All innovation labs located here. Serves the full continuum from Children's House through Adolescent Community. Address: Osman Nagar Road, Osman Nagar, Tellapur, Hyderabad, Telangana 502032.

## The Four Developmental Communities

Toddler Community — Nido and Toddler (Ages 15 months – 3 years): Inner command: "Help me to do it myself." Gentle Settlement — parents stay until child feels safe, no deadline for trust. 46 developmental markers tracked. Octagonal classrooms, 300-acre botanical garden boundary, real wood-glass-metal materials.

Children's House (Ages 2.5–6 | Nursery, PP1, PP2): Inner command: "Help me to think for myself." 3–4 hours uninterrupted work time daily. Writing precedes reading. Children write cursive and read fluently by age 6. Master four operations and fractions before Grade 1. Every material has built-in Control of Error.

Elementary Community (Ages 6–12 | Grades 1–6): Inner command: "Help me to understand for myself." Cosmic Education — five Great Stories of the universe. Going Out programme — self-organised research expeditions across Hyderabad. In 2019, students filed five patent applications with the Indian Patent Office aged 8–12. Laboratories of Character: Karate, Sports, Dance, Arts.

Adolescent Community (Ages 12–18 | Grades 7–10): Inner command: "Help me to think with society." Valorization — the adolescent's need to prove worth through genuine contribution. Micro-Economy: Hydroponics and Terra Eutopia (paper recycling). Cambridge/IGCSE affiliation. SBB-1 CubeSat launched January 2026. Three students presented at 30th AMI International Congress, Mérida (May 2026).

## Innovation Infrastructure (Tellapur Campus)

Innovation Lab: 500 tools across 18 years. Laser cutters, 3D printers, CNC machines. Every project asks: Should I make this? Does it serve life?

Biomimicry Hive: Nature as mentor. Gecko adhesion, termite ventilation, seed dispersal. The Seed Bank. Regenerative engineering — does this give back more than it takes?

Drone Lab: Five patent applications by students aged 8–12 (2019). Conscience Check before every flight: Privacy, Safety, Ecology, Purpose. Named student inventors: Ayushman, Sanshray, Dhairya, Nayonika, Uma, Aditi, Akira, Shourya, Anshul, Nihal, Vivaswath, Aryan, Hasith, Akshat.

Space Lab: SBB-1 CubeSat launched January 11 2026 on ISRO PSLV-C62. 10cm × 10cm — same dimensions as the Pink Tower cube. 17 students aged 12–15. IN-SPACe Authorization: PMA/IN-SPACe/AUTH/2026/115. SBB-2 announced May 2026. Cosmic Responsibility: space is shared commons.

CESDI: Centre for Early-Stage Design Innovation. 1,000 sq ft. Collaboration with IIT Hyderabad Department of Design. IQx Innovation Quotient assessment.

## Architecture

Octagonal classrooms across all environments. Eliminates dead corners, equalises sound distribution, reduces cognitive load, supports Synaptic Fusion. Wooden staircase — hand-crafted, proprioceptive feedback. Biophilic design. 2-acre Montessori Farm with 4–5 half-day visits per year. Risk and Resilience Zones. Multi-Modal Integration drills.
